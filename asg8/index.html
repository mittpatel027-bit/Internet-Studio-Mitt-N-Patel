Discussion with Classmate

While talking to my friend she thought that I definetly include a shortened version of "reference" within the domain name. As reference images are at the center of my website's purpose. Additionally we both agreed that the domain name had to clearly relate to the audience of the website. She suggested I include words like paint or draw, to create a connection to artists who will use the site.

Best Domain Name

The name we liked best was RefStack, as it felt like it was most accurate to both the function and organization of the website. I plan one using Pinterest's system of "Boards" and I think "Stack" is a similar feeling to it. Additionally the name includes a shorting of reference, which is significant to the usage of the website.
