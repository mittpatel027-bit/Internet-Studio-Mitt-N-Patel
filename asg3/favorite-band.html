Silica Gel

Silica Gel is a indie rock band from Korea based in Seoul. The banf currently comprises Kim Han-joo, Kim Geon-jay, Kim Min-su, and Choi Woong-hee. The band chose their name after noticing silica gel in their gum container before an art festival.


I love silica gel because their music is so unique and lyrically compelling. I was first introduced to their music in senior year of highschool. Now whenever I listen to Silica Gel I feel nostalgic about my final year at highschool.
