Where to Eat

Dining

Girl & The Goat

Located in the West Loop, this New American hot spot by chef Stephanie Izard is known for its nose-to-tail cooking and inventive small plates. The open kitchen adds drama to the dining experience, and the menu features bold flavors like wood oven-roasted pig face, goat empanadas, and crispy beef short ribs. It's a favorite for adventurous eaters and foodies alike.

  • Rating: 4.7
  • Price Range: $50-100
  • Address:809 W Randolph St, Chicago, IL

Aba

Aba brings elevated Mediterranean cuisine to a stylish rooftop setting in Fulton Market. With influences from Israel, Lebanon, Turkey, and Greece, the menu includes dishes like lamb ragu hummus, crispy short rib hummus, and skirt steak shawarma. The lounge-style rooftop patio is perfect for cocktails and sunset views.

  • Rating: 4.8
  • Price Range: $50-100
  • Address:302 N Green St 3rd Floor, Chicago, IL
